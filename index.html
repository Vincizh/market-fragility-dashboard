Chart CIs are 90% (5th–95th pct) from a circular block bootstrap of the weekly forward-return series within each band (block length = horizon in weeks to respect overlapping-window autocorrelation, 1500 resamples); bands with N<30 are merged upward for the chart and flagged ⚠ in the table.
